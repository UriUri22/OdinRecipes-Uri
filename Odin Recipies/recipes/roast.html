Japanese Roast

Description

This is a recipe for making a Japanese style roast, this roast is absolutely delectable and is great with a side of rice and broccoli

ingredients

Here are some things you'll need before we start cooking

  • 2 onions, finely chopped
  • 5 cloves of minced garlic
  • 1 beef roast(of course 3 1/2 pounds is reccomended
  • 1 can of onion flavored beef broth(About 14.5 ounces)
  • 1 1/3 cups of rice wine
  • 2/3 cups of soy sauce
  • 2 tablespoons of white sugar
  • Of course seasonings to your taste, I suggest black pepper.

Instructions

Finally lets get cooking, see the steps below to learn how to savor this roast.

  1. Spread onions and garlic on the bottom of your slow cooker
  2. Place the seasoned roast on top of the onions
  3. In a mixing bowl mix cobine: beef broth. rice wine, and soy sauce.
  4. Pour this mixture over the roast and sprinkle black pepper(or choiced seasoning) and sugar on top.
  5. Don't forget to add the ginger slices!
  6. Cover and cook on low (flipping 2-3 time when neccessary) on low heat for about 12 to 18 hours
  7. Uncover, when the meat is pulling with a fork its able to eat.

And that's how you make one of the most delectable roast I've ever tasted in just a few hours! be sure to make sure the meat is tender and juicy or it just wont be as good!
