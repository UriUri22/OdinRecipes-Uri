Japanese beef bowl/Gyudon

Description

This is a recipe for a japanese style beef bowl known as Gyudon, its super tasty and easy to make. Most of the ingredients are hard reccomendations but you are free to make whatever kind of bowl you would like using with delicious recipe.

Ingredients

  • 4 cups of cooked rice
  • 6 tablespoons of 'mirin' or Japanese sweet wine
  • 3 tablespoons soy sauce
  • 2 tablespoons of 'sake' an alchoholic drink
  • 1 onion, cut into wedges
  • 4 eggs
  • 1/2 teaspoon minced peppers or pickled ginger.

Instructions

  1. Divide the cooked rice into four bowls seperately.
  2. In a pot on medium heat, bring: the Mirin, soy sauce, and sake to a boil.
  3. Add onions to the mixture then cook and stir until the onion is translucent, known as carmalizing.
  4. After about 5 minutes place in your beef and simmer until no longer pink and cooked well done.
  5. Using a slotted spoon, place a spoonful or two(based on portion) onto a bowl of rice prepared earlier.
  6. In a seperate pot, place the four eggs into the water and bring to a soft boil(5-7minute cook time.)
  7. When done, peel then, cut them in half and place them on opposite sides of the rice bowl.
  8. Garnish with with pickled ginger or minced pepper and serve!

And with that you can enjoy you Gyudon Rice Bowl! Feel free to style it to meet your taste if neccessary but other than that its axtually not to hard to make!
